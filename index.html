04/08/08: Cookie Cupcakes

My all-time favorite cartoon character has to be Cookie Monster—and my ultimate dessert? Hands down, cupcakes. So when I stumbled across a recipe for cookie-dough-stuffed cupcakes, I nearly lost my mind. As Cookie himself once wisely said, "Sometimes me think, 'What is love?' And then me think love is what last cookie is for. Me give up last cookie for you." I wonder if the same philosophy applies to cupcakes... (Spoiler: I’m keeping every last one!)
